Protecting Your Brand in Jordan

Understand the fundamentals of trademark protection and why it is a critical asset for your business's growth and security in the Jordanian market.

What Can Be Registered?

A trademark is a unique, visually perceptible sign that distinguishes your goods or services. The key is distinctiveness. In Jordan, this can include:

  • ✓**Words, Names, & Slogans:** Including company and personal names.
  • ✓**Logos & Symbols:** Figurative elements and unique designs.
  • ✓**Shapes:** Distinctive product shapes or packaging.
  • ✓**Colors:** Specific colors or combinations that are unique to a brand.
  • ✓**Combinations:** Any mix of the elements above.

Why is Registration Important?

Registration transforms your brand from an idea into a legally protected, valuable business asset. Key benefits include:

  • ✓**Exclusive Legal Rights:** Prevents others from using your mark in Jordan.
  • ✓**Powerful Deterrent:** Discourages counterfeiters and infringers.
  • ✓**Valuable Business Asset:** Can be sold, licensed, or used for financing.
  • ✓**Builds Trust:** Signals professionalism and boosts brand recognition.
  • ✓**Market Expansion:** Establishes your rights for future growth.

Trademark Lifecycle & Management

Once registered, your trademark needs to be maintained and can be leveraged as a business asset. Here's what you need to know about its lifecycle.

Duration & Renewal

10

Years of Protection

Your trademark is protected for a decade and can be renewed indefinitely for subsequent 10-year periods. Renew within the last year to ensure continuous protection.

Use Requirements

3

Years of Non-Use

A mark is vulnerable to cancellation if not genuinely used for three consecutive years. Consistent use is key to maintaining your rights.

Assignment & Licensing

⇄

Leverage Your Asset

Your trademark can be sold (assigned) or you can permit others to use it (license). These agreements must be recorded to be legally effective.

Enforcing Your Rights

Registration is your shield. Here’s how you can actively defend your brand against infringement in Jordan.

Monitoring

Actively watch the market and Official Gazette for similar marks.

Cease & Desist

The first step is often a formal letter demanding the infringer stop.

Legal Action

File civil or criminal complaints to seek damages and stop infringement.

Customs Measures

Record your mark with customs to seize counterfeit goods at the border.

International Considerations

Jordan is part of a global intellectual property framework. Understand how international treaties can benefit your trademark strategy.

Paris Convention

Allows you to claim a "priority right." If you file in Jordan within 6 months of filing in another member country, your Jordanian application is treated as if filed on the earlier date.

Well-Known Marks

Jordan offers protection to famous international marks, even if not registered locally, preventing confusingly similar registrations.

Seeking Professional Advice

Navigating trademark law can be complex. Engaging a local professional is the most effective way to protect your rights.

Why Hire an Expert?

A qualified Jordanian trademark agent or lawyer can handle every aspect of the process, from searches and filing to responding to office actions, managing renewals, and leading enforcement actions, ensuring your brand is secure.

Key Authorities in Jordan

  • Ministry of Industry, Trade, and Supply (MIT)
    The primary government body.
  • Industrial Property Protection Directorate (IPPD)
    The department that handles registrations.
  • Jordanian Customs Department
    Your partner in border enforcement.
